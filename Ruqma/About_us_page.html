Journey Has Begin With Ruqma
We are four classmates who came together to create Ruqma a platform that provides digital services in a simple and modern way. Our goal is to make it easier for people to offer and access quality digital services anytime, anywhere. Through Ruqma, we’ve built a space where service providers can share their work, manage their services, and connect with clients — all through one easy to use system. Working on Ruqma has been more than just a project for us it’s been a learning journey that taught us teamwork, creativity, and innovation.
